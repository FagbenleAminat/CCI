ABOUT US

OUR VISION

“A world where all orphaned and vulnerable children and youth in Africa have access to quality care, education, and support, enabling them to become confident, resilient, and productive members of society.”

Inspring young minds, one young mind at a time.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is “To provide comprehensive care, education, and opportunities for orphaned and vulnerable children and youth in Africa, empowering them to reach their full potential and become self-sufficient members of society.”

Inspring young minds, one young mind at a time.

UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION (UVP)

Chrysalis Care Initiative distinguishes itself through its innovative approach to addressing the complex challenges faced by orphaned and vulnerable children. By combining cutting-edge programs with a deep understanding of local contexts, we aim to achieve exceptional outcomes and create sustainable impact.

CORE VALUES

Compassion:

Demonstrating empathy and care for the children we serve.

Excellence:

Striving for the highest standards in our programs and operations.

Innovation:

Embracing new approaches to address the challenges faced by orphaned and other vulnerable children.

Sustainability:

Building programs that have a long-lasting impact on communities.

Accountability:

Being transparent and responsible in our stewardship of resources.

Partnership:

Collaborating with governments, communities and other organizations to achieve our goals.

Target Beneficiaries
  • Orphan
  • Children living in poverty.
  • Children living with disability
  • Children afflicted by conflict or natural disaster
  • Youth transitioning out of care.

Geographic Scope

Africa-wide with a focus on countries with high orphans and vulnerable child populations.
