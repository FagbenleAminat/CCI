FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
Q. How does CCI help orphanages?

A. We provide emotional, physical, and educational support to children in orphanages.

Q. How can my orphanage benefit from CCI?

A. We offer therapy, skills training, and education programs for the children in your care.

Q.Can orphans receive therapy from CCI?

A.Yes, we provide therapy to address mental and emotional health.

Q.What kind of therapy is available?

A. We offer one-on-one counseling, group therapy, and mental health support.

Q.How does CCI engage with communities?

A.We work with local communities and stakeholders to build sustainable programs.

Q. How can my community partner with CCI?

A. Reach out to collaborate on programs that align with our mission.

Q. How does CCI support the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)?

A. We contribute to goals like health, education, and reducing inequality through our programs.

Q.How do I choose the right volunteer program?

A. We match you with a program based on your skills and interests.
