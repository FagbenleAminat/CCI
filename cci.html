Inspiring futures, one young mind at a time:

Chrysalis Care Initiative (CCI) is a pioneering organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable children and adolescents across Africa.

Building Emotional Resilience

We provide emotional resilience, education, and empowerment to help children thrive in orphanages and challenging environments.

Fostering a Bright Future

Our goal is to ensure every child has the opportunity to live a fulfilling life through innovation, community engagement, and a holistic approach.

OUR IMPACT

Impacting Over 150 Stakeholders:
We have positively influenced over 150 caregivers, parents, and educators. We're proud of the meaningful change we've created and are committed to continuing our efforts to revolutionize care for vulnerable young minds.
